Shipping & Logistics Services

Our Shipping Vessel operates providing shipping services through PNG waters
IBK Ship does the following services:
Cargo Shipping
Passenger sea transport
Wharf Handling and Coordination
Hire Basis
Vessel Key Features:
IBK is a 48 metre long, 9.5 metre wide two-deck, nine transverse bulkhead vessel that can carry passengers and cargo. The vessel is equipped with modern communications, safety equipment and has 86 passenger seats in the top deck. The vessel has a gross tonnage of316 tonnes and a net tonnage of 94 tonnes and determined in accordance with the provision of the international convention of tonnage measurements of ships, 1969, under the Authority of the Government of Papua New Guinea. The Ship also has freezing facility for storage    
Impact of the Vessel:During the launching of the vessel, the Prime Minister of Papua New Guinea, Hon. James Marape, described the development as "a symbol of unity and economic engine" for the region. IBK can connect local produce of crabs, seafood, sago , and others to urban markets...

Bore Water Systems

Bore Water is a major specialty of our business. We conduct initial water detection through to delivering water to users and communities.
Site Assessment and Hydrogeological Studies: We evaluate project feasibility by conducting comprehensive analysis of Georgical and hydrological factors, ensuring the viability of bore water supply initiatives.     
Drilling and Installation: Our expert team provides drilling services for bore wells down to 45-70 metres depth to access crystal clear drinking water and handles the installation of pumps, pipelines, and water storage facilities with precision and efficiency    
Water Quality Monitoring and Treatment: We prioritise the safety of drinking water by implementing regular monitoring programs and employing effective treatment solutions to maintain high-quality standards.    
Community Engagement and Capacity Building: We engage with local communities to understand their specific needs and provide tailored training and support for sustainable water management practices, fostering community involment and empowerment. Through our bore water supply programs, we are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life and driving economic development in underserved areas and communities of Papua New Guinea.
